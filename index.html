Welcome

Welcome to the Yelverton Bowmen, a friendly archery club based in Plymouth who would like to welcome men, women and children (aged 12+) from Plymouth and surrounding areas to the ancient sport of Archery.
The Shooting Times page provides directions and times for the club.
If you are a member of another club and would like to shoot with us as a guest, please see our Costs page.
We recommend new and existing club members visit the Records room to find the club records you want to break next, find out more info about the schemes and competitions we run, or download the latest Rounds and Classifications sheets so you can start earning those classifcations and badges.
Our Useful Links page provides details of websites that can help with your Acrhery progress.

Beginners

New to archery? Want to learn but don’t know where to start? Well with our one off taster sessions and 6 week beginners course we’ve got you covered. Anyone can get involved from children to senior citizens, disabled and able bodied. Whatever your situation our qualified coaches will help you get up and running. Take a look at our beginners section for more details.
